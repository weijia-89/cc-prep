# ISC2 CC Practice Exams - Mocks 1 and 2

Two full-length 100-question mocks. Each weighted to match the real ISC2 CC exam distribution (D1 26, D2 10, D3 22, D4 24, D5 18). Mocks 3-4 (weighted toward Wei's weak domains 2 and 4) live in `PRACTICE_EXAMS_2.md`.

**How to use:** 2 hours per mock, no notes. Answer all 100. Score yourself with the key at the end of each mock. Target 70+ for confidence; below 65 means re-study the missed domain.
